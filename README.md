# BioFlorida landing page

This page is a prospect-facing, single-file static brief prepared for BioFlorida, the Florida state life sciences trade association. It is built in BioFlorida's own institutional register (navy primary, green and orange accents, Open Sans) so that when Matt hands Jake's phone to Mark Glickman or the workforce committee chair at the booth, the page reads as a document their association could have produced itself. Its job is to move one conversation forward: a thirty-minute call with Matt Creamer to decide whether a Workforce Development methodology handoff, a three-city chapter cycle, or a 2027 conference session is the right shape for an Eduba / BioFlorida partnership. The page leads with BioFlorida's own numbers (8,600 establishments, 107,000 Floridians, seven chapters), frames Eduba's 60 / 30 / 10 orchestration thesis against the regulated-industry reality member companies live in, produces the Correlation One case study and Ethics Engine paper as the defensibility evidence, and lands on a single Calendly CTA. No pricing. No retainer terms. No revenue-share figures. Voice is understated, active, and free of em dashes, antithesis patterns, and performed questions.
